Do You Have Questions?

Below you’ll find answers to the most common questions you may have on Brands & Vines. If you can’t
find the answer you’re looking for, just Contact us!

Where do you deliver?

We offer delivery in Dubai, Sharjah, Ajman, Abu Dhabi

Can I specify a date/time for my order to be delivered?

Yes , but there will be an extra charge.

How will I know when the flower has been delivered?

Confirmation through Whatsapp/call.

What is the lifespan of fresh flowers?

It will vary on flowers. For Roses, it takes 5-7 days, for Special Flowers (Holland), it takes 3-5 days.

What happens in case the recipient is not available at the time of delivery?

We will leave the order at the reception /in front of his/her door (as per the receiver).

How can I be sure that the flowers arrive in good condition?

We take photos before and upon the delivery.
